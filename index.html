Launch Curriculum

Launch is built from the ground-up to serve the needs of young career starters wanting to enter technical careers. Designed as a longer program to accommodate a rigorous technical curriculum and a heavy emphasis on professional development, graduates will be enterprise-ready C#/.NET software developers.
